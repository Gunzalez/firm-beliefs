Charities

It's about giving back!

We'll be honest - we don't really like the term not for profit! It implies loss making... But we use it for the moment until we find something we like better.

Our services are geared towards individuals and organisations that want to make a social impact. That of course is not restricted to those of you in 'civil society' or 'the third sector' but we find that most of those who seek our help are those who are set up as charities, followed by social enterprises who seek to retain the profits they do make within their organisation.

We are proud to support the ethos and aims of ACEVO as a corporate member.

Who we give to

We help you to position yourselves within your market place - as well as in other market places. There is no one market place any more for what you want to achieve. You are now facing competition from for-profits who aim to make a social impact, just like you do.

We help you to think broadly and strategically, as well as deliver on the goals you set yourself; you may find your scope for influence and impact will change.

We also help you to concentrate on your effectiveness - by improving and enhancing how you use your people, your stakeholders, your operational systems and your finances to achieve what you need to. And that includes working with trustees, purchasers of services, and all those who can influence the success of the future of the charity.

How we donate

Just as with our 'commercial profit making clients', we work to ensure that you can do what we can do. That you know what we know. External consultants should not hold the monopoly on knowing how to make good charities into excellent ones! You can just get us in to help with HR or Finance or Trustee performance or good governance - but it seems to us that you should also have those skills in-house. So we help you to develop the skills as we work on the projects with you.
